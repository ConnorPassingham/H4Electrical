Who We Are

We believe the smallest things make the biggest difference.

H4 Electrical is your trusted local partner for all your electrical needs. We guide our clients from the very beginning to the end of their projects — from the initial design stages of new builds to the safe and professional rewiring of existing properties.

We also proudly support contractors with expert electrical requirements for small to medium building projects. Our customer-first mindset ensures that we deliver reliable, high-quality workmanship every single time.
